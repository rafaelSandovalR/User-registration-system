Q: What can I do if I forget my user name and/or password?
A: There is a button on the lggin page to reset your user name and/ or password.

Q: As a student what should I do if I am not satisfied with a mentor advice?
A: If you are not satified with a mentor's advice, then you can always reschedule with another mentor.

Q: What to do if I did not receive a confirmation email from the mentee for my appointment?
A: If for any reason you did not receive the confirmation email, you can always show up for the appointment or email us about the issue.

Q: As a student, how do I rate my mentor?
A: After every meeting you will be prompted a window to rate your mentor.

Q: What can I do if for any reason I miss my appointment?
A: If you do not show up after 5 mintues for a metting, then it will be canceled and unfortunately you have to reschedule.

Q: If I am having trouble to solve a Mathematic, Physics, Chemistry, or any subject problem, then I am willingly to find help?
A: We will always have very bright students available until 9pm from Monday to Friday to help other students out with their homework.

Q: As a student, how can I become a mentor?
A: For the students who would like to become a mentor, we will have a form available on the site to fill out after that you submit that form to your faculty Chair Person.

Q: Will I have to pay for the mentoring?
A: Absolute not! The service is free.
